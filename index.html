About Me

Born at Berkeley Alta Bates in 2002, Elias Lehman lives in the East San Fransisco Bay Area. In 2014, he took a course in the UC Berkeley Valley Life Sciences Building, begining his journey on campus. It wasn't until 2020 that Elias made Berkeley his formal academic institution and began studying Physics and Electrical Engineering.

Elias's research spans from quantum-limited amplifiers to cosmology with machine learning. He currently working with the Advanced Quantum Testbed at Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory and the Bay Area Envionmental Research Institute.
