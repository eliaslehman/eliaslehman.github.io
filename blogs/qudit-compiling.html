Compiling Quantum Circuits in Higher Dimensions

Throughout this work, our Hilbert space will be of constant prime dimension $d$ with orthonormal basis $\{\ket{q}\}_{q\in \mathbb{Z}_d}$ where $\mathbb{Z}_d$ is the field of integers modulo $d$. We begin by introducing the generalized Pauli matrices known as the clock and shift operators $X$ and $Z$: \[X = \sum_{q=0}^{d-1}|q\rangle\langle q+1|\ \ \ \ \ \ \ Z = \sum_{q=0}^{d-1}\tau^{q^2}|q\rangle\langle q|\]
