About Me

Born at Berkeley Alta Bates in 2002, I have lived in the East San Fransisco Bay Area for nearly 22 years. I first experienced UC Berekeley in Summer, 2014 when I took a class in the Valley Life Sciences Building. It wasn't until 2020 that I made Berkeley my formal academic institution and began my career as a Physicist. I am currently in my last year as an undergraduate. My education and research span the widest scales, from quantum mechanical systems such as quantum-limited amplifiers and superconducting quantum bits, to applications of machine learning to study the expansion of the universe. I am currently working with the Advanced Quantum Testbed at Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory and the Bay Area Envionmental Research Institute.
